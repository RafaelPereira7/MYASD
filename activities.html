School Activities

Discover ways to get involved through clubs and organizations at ASD. This page highlights meaningful volunteering opportunities that give you service hours, build and showcase leadership, and strengthen our community.

Why Get Involved

Participating can also show college admission officers if you're a leader, someone who takes initiative, and is passionate for the things you do. If you participate in even just a few clubs, take internships, or lead activities, you help build a picture of who you are. These experiences give you examples of what to write about in essays, can help your teachers write their letters of rec, and allow you to represent our school.
